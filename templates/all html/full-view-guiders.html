About Me

Sharing is who I am, and guiding is where I am at my best, because I've been on both sides of that equation, and getting to deliver useful guiding is my meaningful way to be a part of the creative community. I've spent a long time watching others nature, and village, to refine how I work with you to be efficient, useful and, most importantly, memorable. I want you to carry what I've shown you into a bright future. I have a wife (a lovely Irish girl) and kids. I have lived and worked in many places (as Kiwis tend to do) – but most of my 14+ years of working a guiders has had one overriding theme: bringing others along for the ride as we all try to change the world with our stories, our labours of love and our art. I'm a certified Tourist Guiders in Bangladesh. I've been starting for 14+ years. I come from being a media designer and content creator – so I understand exactly where you're at now. I've been there. I love this stuff. Print, digital publishing, web and video. I can see how it all connects. And I can see how we can share those connections.

We earned a reputation of being good at what we do. Let us take your online shop to new dimension in success!
